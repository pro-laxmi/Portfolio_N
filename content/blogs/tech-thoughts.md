## The Intersection of AI and Storage

Building systems like ChiralDB and my Multimodal RAG project has taught me a lot about how databases need to evolve to support modern AI workloads.

### Vector Databases
Traditional relational databases are no longer sufficient when you need to perform semantic search over unstructured data. This is where tools like **Qdrant** and **ChromaDB** really shine.
