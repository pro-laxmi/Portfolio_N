### Project Overview

Benchmarked 10+ computer vision architectures, including LeNet, VGG, ResNet, DenseNet, MobileNet, EfficientNet, etc. Implemented a Vision Transformer (ViT) from scratch in PyTorch to compare attention mechanisms against standard convolutions. Analysed accuracy vs. compute trade-offs on MNIST dataset.
